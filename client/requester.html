Company Policy

OTES is a platform that connects Task Requesters with Task Performers for paid task execution. All users must use the system in a respectful, honest, and responsible way.

Task Requesters

  • Provide clear task details, including description, requirements, location, deadline, and payment amount.
  • Review completed work and approve it only when the task matches the agreed requirements.

Task Performers

  • Accept only tasks they are able to complete.
  • Update task progress and communicate with the Requester when needed.

Payments and Receipts

  • All payments must be handled through the system.
  • Payment is transferred after task completion and approval.
  • A receipt is generated after a successful payment.

Communication

  • Users must use the task chat for questions, updates, clarifications, and task-related information.
  • Users must not share false information, inappropriate content, private payment details, or personal contact information outside the required workflow.

Reports and Admin Review

  • Users can submit a report for payment issues, incomplete tasks, user problems, or system issues.
  • Reports are reviewed by the Admin.
  • The Admin may monitor task activity, check payment issues, and restrict users when needed to keep the platform safe and reliable.
